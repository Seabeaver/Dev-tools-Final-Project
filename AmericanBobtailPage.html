    • American Bobtail Cat Page

    Description:

    American bobtails have large almond-shaped eyes and can come in any color. They have long back legs and a large, athletic body that typically weighs between 7 & 16 pounds. These cats are sometimes slow maturing and can take longer than your typical cat to reach full adulthood, around 2 to 3 years.
